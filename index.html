Massive City Planning & Build Tutorial

Video by: Creator | Guide by: BuildItApp Team

CHALLENGE ACCEPTED! This is a huge undertaking. Patience, attention to detail, and lots of resources are key. Pause and rewatch frequently!

Build Overview

Embark on the colossal task of planning, laying out, and constructing elements of a sprawling, detailed city. This tutorial covers principles and examples for creating your own urban masterpiece.

Difficulty: EXTREMELY Advanced / Mega-Project

Materials: Varies wildly; Concrete, Stone, Glass, Wood, Roads, Greenspace (Refer to video for specific building examples)

Build Phases (General Outline):

  1. Phase 1: City Planning, Layout & Road Network
  2. Phase 2: Core Infrastructure & Utilities (Simulated)
  3. Phase 3: Constructing Building Archetypes (Residential, Commercial, Civic)
  4. Phase 4: Landscaping, Parks & Public Spaces
  5. Phase 5: Detailing, Lighting & Bringing the City to Life

Phase 1: City Planning, Layout & Road Network

Focus on the macro-level design: defining districts, planning the main road grid, and considering terrain.

Step 1.1: Conceptualize Your City

Decide on the city's theme (modern, medieval, futuristic), size, and key districts (downtown, residential, industrial, parks).

Step 1.2: Outline Main Road Network

Using temporary blocks, lay out the primary roads and highways. Consider a grid, organic, or hybrid pattern.

Step 1.3: Define District Zones

Roughly mark out the areas for your different districts based on the road network.

Phase 2: Core Infrastructure & Utilities (Simulated)

Add elements that suggest a functioning city, like power lines, water features, and transportation hubs (if not covered by other builds).

Step 2.1: Simulate Power Grid

Place "power stations" (can be decorative builds) and run "power lines" (fences, iron bars on poles) if fitting the theme.

Step 2.2: Incorporate Water Features

Plan for rivers, canals, or a waterfront if your city design includes them. Build bridges.

Phase 3: Constructing Building Archetypes

Build a few example buildings for each district to establish the architectural style. You won't build every single one in a tutorial, but show the method.

Step 3.1: Example Residential Building(s)

Construct a small apartment block or a few houses typical of your residential zone.

Step 3.2: Example Commercial Building(s)

Build a shop, an office front, or a small market stall for your commercial district.

Step 3.3: Example Civic Building(s)

Construct a small town hall, library, or police station as a civic building example.

Phase 4: Landscaping, Parks & Public Spaces

Integrate green spaces, parks, plazas, and other public areas to break up the urban density.

Step 4.1: Design and Build a Central Park or Plaza

Create a significant public space with paths, trees, fountains, and seating.

Step 4.2: Add Street-Level Landscaping

Line streets with trees, add small planters, or create pocket parks.

Phase 5: Detailing, Lighting & Bringing the City to Life

Add small details that make the city feel inhabited and functional. Implement a city-wide lighting scheme.

Step 5.1: Street Furniture and Details

Add streetlights, benches, traffic lights (decorative), mailboxes, etc.

Step 5.2: City-Wide Lighting Plan

Ensure roads, public spaces, and building exteriors are appropriately lit for nighttime aesthetics.

Step 5.3: Populate (Optional)

If desired, use armor stands or villager setups (if you're ambitious) to simulate population in certain areas.
