Serene Japanese Pagoda Tutorial

Video by: Creator | Guide by: BuildItApp Team

Build Overview

Construct an elegant multi-tiered Japanese Pagoda with iconic curved roofs and intricate wooden details. This tutorial follows the video guide provided.

Difficulty: Intermediate-Advanced

Materials: Wood (Dark Oak, Spruce), Stone, Lanterns (Refer to video)

Build Phases (General Outline):

  1. Phase 1: Foundation & First Tier Base
  2. Phase 2: First Tier Walls & Roof
  3. Phase 3: Subsequent Tiers
  4. Phase 4: Top Spire & Details

Phase 1: Foundation & First Tier Base

Follow the video (approx. 0:00-X:XX) to lay out the pagoda's base, establish the core pillar structure, and build the platform for the first main tier.

Step 1.1: Mark Out the Base Dimensions

Using your chosen primary block, mark out the square or rectangular footprint of the pagoda as shown in the initial part of the video.

Step 1.2: Construct Main Support Pillars

Erect the main vertical support pillars at the corners and other key points identified in the video.

Phase 2: First Tier Walls & Roof

This phase covers building the walls for the first tier and constructing its elaborate, curved roof (approx. X:XX-Y:YY in video).

Step 2.1: Build First Tier Walls

Connect the pillars with wall sections, incorporating any window or door designs shown in the video guide.

Step 2.2: Create the First Roof Eaves and Curve

Carefully follow the video instructions to build the overhanging eaves and achieve the signature upward curve of the pagoda roof using stairs and slabs.

Phase 3: Subsequent Tiers

Repeat the process for the second, third, and any additional tiers. Each upper tier is typically a smaller version of the one below it, maintaining the pagoda's tapering profile (approx. Y:YY-Z:ZZ in video).

Step 3.1: Build Second Tier Platform and Walls

Construct the base for the second tier on top of the first roof, then build its walls.

Phase 4: Top Spire & Details

Complete the pagoda by constructing the topmost spire (finial) and adding decorative details such as lanterns, railings, banners, and any interior touches shown (approx. Z:ZZ-End in video).

Step 4.1: Construct the Spire

Build the intricate spire that crowns the pagoda, following the video's design.
