Majestic Japanese Pagoda Tutorial

By: BuildItApp Team

Build Overview

Learn to construct this serene and intricate Japanese Pagoda. Our detailed guide breaks down every layer, from foundation to rooftop, inspired by traditional designs.

Difficulty: Intermediate

Estimated Build Time: 4-6 hours

Dimensions (approx): 25W x 25L x 40H blocks

Minecraft Version: Java 1.18+ / Bedrock

Required Materials (Example)

  • Dark Oak Wood: ~8 Stacks
  • Spruce Wood Planks: ~6 Stacks
  • Stone Bricks: ~10 Stacks
  • Cobblestone: ~5 Stacks
  • Dark Oak Stairs & Slabs
  • Spruce Stairs & Slabs
  • Lanterns: ~30
  • Dark Oak Fences & Gates

Phase 1: Foundation & First Floor Layout

We'll start by laying out the base of the pagoda and building up the first main floor.

Step 1.1: Outline the Base

Using Stone Bricks, create a 21x21 block square outline on flat ground. This will be the outermost edge of your foundation.

Step 1.2: Raise Foundation Platform

Fill the outlined area with Cobblestone, raising it 1 block high. Then add another layer of Stone Bricks on top of the Cobblestone.

Step 1.3: Place Corner Pillars

At each corner of the Stone Brick platform, place Dark Oak Wood Logs 5 blocks high.

Tip: Ensure your ground is level before starting for best results.

Phase 2: Second Floor & Lower Roof

Now we'll construct the second story and the iconic sloped roof for the first tier.

Step 2.1: First Floor Walls

Connect the Dark Oak pillars with Spruce Wood Planks to form the walls of the first floor. Leave openings for doors and windows as desired.

Step 2.2: Lower Roof Eaves

Using Dark Oak Stairs and Slabs, begin creating the overhanging eaves of the first roof tier. This typically extends 2-3 blocks out from the walls.
